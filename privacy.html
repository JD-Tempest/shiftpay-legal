ShiftPay Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 Mar 2026

ShiftPay is designed to respect your privacy.

Data Collection

ShiftPay stores shift information entered by the user, such as hours worked, pay rates, overtime details, and notes.

Local Storage

Your shift data is stored locally on your device. ShiftPay does not upload your shift data to external servers.

Payments

If you subscribe to ShiftPay Pro, payments are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play. ShiftPay does not store your payment information.

Analytics

ShiftPay does not collect personal analytics data at this time.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at: your@email.com
